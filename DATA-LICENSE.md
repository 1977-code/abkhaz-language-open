## Why CC0

These files are **aggregated frequency statistics** — factual measurements of how often
word-forms and lemmas occur in a corpus. Facts are not copyrightable, and we want this
data to be maximally reusable by teachers, linguists, lexicographers, and other
minority-language projects.

## Important: sources are not relicensed

CC0 covers **our aggregated output**, not the underlying corpora. The data is derived from
open sources (the Abkhaz National Corpus, Mozilla Common Voice, Abkhaz Wikipedia) whose own
licenses continue to apply to *their* materials. We do **not** redistribute raw third-party
corpus exports in this repository; the scripts in `06_corpus/scripts/` let you regenerate
data from the original sources. Provenance and source licenses are documented in
[`ATTRIBUTION.md`](ATTRIBUTION.md).
