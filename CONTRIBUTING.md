# Contributing

Thank you for helping build an open future for Abkhaz (аҧсуа бызшәа). This is an
under-resourced, endangered language — **small, expert corrections are extremely valuable.**

## Who we need most

### 🗣️ Native speakers & teachers
You don't need to be a programmer. The most useful help is reviewing draft material for
**naturalness and errors**:
- Draft lessons: [`08_textbook/chapters/`](08_textbook/chapters/)
- Word lists / glosses: [`data/`](data/) and the review guides in
  [`05_methodology/NATIVE_REVIEW_GUIDE.md`](05_methodology/NATIVE_REVIEW_GUIDE.md)

Open an issue titled `native-review: <file>` and tell us what's wrong or unnatural — even a
single word or example is a real contribution.

### 📚 Linguists & Abkhaz-studies scholars
- Audit the **frequency lists** and part-of-speech tagging (`06_corpus/`, `data/freq_*.csv`).
- Review the **grammar scope** and verb-affix guide (`04_language_standard/`).
- See [`05_methodology/SCIENTIFIC_REVIEW_GUIDE.md`](05_methodology/SCIENTIFIC_REVIEW_GUIDE.md).

### 💻 Developers
- Improve the reproducible pipeline in `06_corpus/scripts/`.
- Improve the website in `09_platform/`.
- Add data validation, tests, or tooling for the CSVs.

### 🌍 Translators
Help mirror materials across RU ↔ EN (and other languages) to widen access.

## Ground rules

1. **Respect rights.** Do not add third-party text, audio, scans, or dictionary entries
   unless they are openly licensed or cleared. When in doubt, ask in an issue. See
   [`03_rights/CONTENT_LICENSE_POLICY.md`](03_rights/CONTENT_LICENSE_POLICY.md).
2. **Mark uncertainty.** Draft material is *pending native/academic review*. Keep that
   honesty — flag what is verified vs. proposed.
3. **Licensing of contributions.** By contributing, you agree your contribution is released
   under the repository's licenses (code → MIT, content → CC BY 4.0, data → CC0).

## How to contribute

- **Small fix:** open an issue, or a pull request with a clear description.
- **Larger work:** open an issue first so we can coordinate and avoid duplicate effort.

Мы будем рады помощи и на русском — открывайте issue на любом удобном языке.
